About the Database

The National Pollutant Release Inventory (NPRI) is Canada's public register of pollutants. This exhibit helps you explore industrial impacts in our environment through data visualization.

How to Explore

1

Choose Tiles

Pick one tile from each category in the bins. Each tile acts as a specific search filter.

2

Place in Slots

Match the geometric shapes on your tiles to the corresponding slots on the table.

3

View Results

The screen will automatically generate a custom report based on your unique combination.
